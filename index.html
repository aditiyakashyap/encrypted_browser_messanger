    Welcome to k-chat 🔒

    A truly serverless, peer-to-peer secure messenger.

    • 🛡️ Zero Logs: We don't have a database. Your messages are mathematically impossible for us to read or store.
    • 🔑 Military E2EE: Every payload is locked inside an AES-256 Vault directly on your device.
    • 👻 Total Anonymity: No phone numbers, no emails, no accounts. Just your private cryptographic ID.

    How to Start:

    1. Click the 🔗 icon next to your ID to send a direct invite link to a friend.
    2. Or, click the + button and paste your friend's ID to connect.
    3. Keep the tab open to ensure the secure tunnel remains active!
